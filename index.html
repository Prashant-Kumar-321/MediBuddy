Frequently Asked Questions by our user !!

  • What types of health issues can Doctor help with?

    Doctor is a general physician with experience in treating a wide range of common health issues, including colds, infections, digestive problems, minor injuries, allergies, and more. For specialized issues, he provides referrals and guidance.

  • Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

    It's recommended to book an appointment in advance to ensure Doctor can dedicate enough time for your consultation. However, walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

  • How can I book an appointment with Doctor?

    You can easily book an appointment by calling the clinic directly, visiting the mediBuddy website's appointment page, or through our partner health app.

  • What should I bring to my first appointment?

    For your first visit, please bring any relevant medical records, a list of medications you are currently taking, and any previous lab reports. This will help Dr. Sharma understand your health background better.

  • Does Doctor offer telemedicine consultations?

    Yes, Doctor offers telemedicine consultations for patients who prefer remote appointments. Simply contact us to schedule a video or phone consultation.

  • What payment methods are accepted?

    We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, and most digital payment platforms. If you have specific insurance coverage, please inquire with us beforehand.
